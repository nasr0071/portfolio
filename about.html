GET TO KNOW ME

Hello! I'm Muriel Nasrallah, a 20-year-old student enrolled in the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College. Ottawa, my hometown, has been the backdrop to my journey, where I've been fortunate to embrace my Lebanese heritage while pursuing my passion for design and technology.

Having previously attended Carleton University for civil engineering, I've transitioned my focus to the creative realm, drawing inspiration from my Lebanese upbringing and education at Franco-Cité, a French-Canadian high school. Fluent in English, French, and Arabic, I navigate seamlessly between languages, enriching my multicultural perspective. In my leisure moments, you'll often find me immersed in the art of drawing, where I indulge in bringing portraits and anime characters to life, channeling my passion for visual expression.
